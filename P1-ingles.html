Ingredients

3 paq. of 190g of Cream Cheese Philadelphia
½ stick of butter at room temperature
1 tbsp of orange zest
1 ½ tza. of white sugar

Bread

1 tza. of vegetable oil
4 pcs of egg
1 tbsp of liquid vanilla
3 cups. of grated carrot
½ tza. of pineapple in finely chopped syrup
2 cups. of wheat flour
1 tbsp of Royal
1 cdita of bicarbonate
1 tbsp of cinnamon powder
1 ½ tza. of sugar
1 ½ tza. chopped walnut

Elaboration

Bread

1. Mix the oil, egg, liquid vanilla, carrot and pineapple.
2.Mix the flour, Royal, baking soda, cinnamon, sugar and nuts in another container.
3. Lightly bathe both mixtures and pour into a cake pan. Bake at 180 ° for 60 minutes or until cooked.

Cheese betun

1. Cream the Philadelphia Cream Cheese with the butter.
2.Add the orange zest and sugar.

Assembly

1. Slice the bread into two layers and place the cheese bitumen between the two layers of bread.
2. Cover the whole cake and decorate with walnut.

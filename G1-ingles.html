Ingredients 230 g of oat flakes
100 g of flour
150 g of brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon of chemical yeast
1 pinch of salt
2 eggs
150 ml extra virgin olive oil
1 teaspoon vanilla or hazelnut essence

Elaboration: In a bowl we put the rolled oats, the flour, the brown sugar, the yeast and the salt, and we mix everything. In another container we mix the eggs, the oil and the essence and incorporate it into the previous mixture. We mix everything, I have only used to mix a spatula, no mixer or blender is needed.
Once everything is mixed, we form balls that we flatten with our hands and put them on a baking tray.
We bake at 180ºC, it must be preheated, about 12 minutes or until you see the edges are golden.
We take them out and leave them on a grid. Once they cool, they will finish hardening.

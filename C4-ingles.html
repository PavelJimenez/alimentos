Ingredients

1 package of Philadelphia Cream Cheese in Slices
1 tza. of blackberry jam
red fruits to decorate

For the crepe

1 tza. of flour
3 tbsp of sugar
1 pinch of salt
1 Â½ tzas. of milk
2 eggs
1 tbsp of vanilla essence
2 tbsp of melted butter

Elaboration

1.Blend the ingredients of the crepes and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
2.Pour a little mixture of crepes, spread, turn and let cook in a hot pan.
3.Fill each crepe with blackberry jam and a slice of Philadelphia Cream Cheese; bend in triangle.
4.Serve and decorate with icing sugar and red berries.

Ingredients

Cake

¾ cup of butter without salt
1 ½ cup granulated sugar
3 eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla essence
1 cup of milk
2 ¼ cup flour
2 teaspoons of baking powder
½ teaspoon of salt

Bitumen

2 cups vegetable shortening
4 ½ cups of sugar glass
½ tablespoon of meringue powder (optional)
½ teaspoon vanilla essence
¼ cup of milk

Elaboration

1. Top the butter on medium speed for 3 minutes until fluffy.
2.Add the little sugar while stirring. In the same way add the eggs, one by one.
3.Mix flour with baking powder and salt.
4. Transfer the dry ingredients and add 1/3 to the blender.
5.Combine the milk with the essence of vanilla, add half to the mixture, then another third of the dried and repeated.
6. Bake until all the ingredients are well incorporated.
7. Grease and flour a cake mold, pour the mixture and bake at 180 ° C for 30 minutes or until, when a toothpick is inserted in the center, it comes out clean.
8. Remove from the oven, let cool for 20 minutes, unmold and cool completely.
9. Place the shortening in the blender at medium speed for 6 minutes until no lumps remain.
10.Pass the icing sugar in a sieve, add it to the butter little by little together with the powdered meringue, vanilla essence and milk.
11. Mix until you have a smooth and creamy bitumen.
12. Decorate the vanilla cake with the bitumen. You can cut the cake in half and place a thin layer of bitumen between each one.

Ingredients

1 cup of flour
1 cup of sugar
1/4 cup cocoa powder
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
3/4 cup of milk
1/4 cup of soft butter
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1 egg
1/4 teaspoon of salt
1 1/2 cups of icing sugar for icing
125 grams of chocolate for glaze
3 tablespoons of butter for glaze
3 tablespoons of water for glaze

Elaboration

1. In a bowl combine flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Stir at low speed with the blender.
2. Add milk, butter and vanilla. Beat with a mixer at low speed, then beat 2 minutes at medium speed, add egg and beat 2 more minutes. Put in a round mold of 22 cms. in diameter, greased and floured.
3. Bake at 350ÂºF for 30 or 35 minutes. Remove from the oven and cool on a rack for 10 minutes. Remove from the mold and let cool completely.
4.To make the glaze: melt chocolate and butter over low heat, remove from the heat and add the icing sugar and hot water always stirring. If it is necessary to add more water to have the consistency of glaze.
5.Glaze the cake and let drain on the sides to cover it completely

Ingredients - 1 cup of flour
- 1 cup of milk
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon of butter
- 1 tablespoon of sugar (optional)
- 1 tablespoon of vanilla (optional)

For the filling:

- Tomato sauce
- Pepperoni, mushrooms or ham
- Manchego cheese
- Basil (optional)

Elaboration: 1. Bake or blend all the ingredients of the dough until there are no lumps, if you prefer you can sift the flour to make it thinner.
2.Preheat a pan and add a little oil or butter so that the crepe does not stick. Once the pan is ready, add a spoonful of the mixture and spread it well so that it is very thin.
3.When you notice that it is already cooked from the bottom, turn it over and add tomato sauce to the middle of the crepe, then the cheese, the pepperoni, the mushrooms or the ham and close it in half. Place it on a plate and sprinkle a little basil.

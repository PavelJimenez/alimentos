Ingredients 1/4 cup of sugar
2 teaspoons of cinnamon
1 1/2 cup of sugar
2 1/2 cup flour
1/2 cup of bitter chocolate powder, sugar free
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/4 teaspoon of salt
225 grams of unsalted butter, at room temperature
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Elaboration: 1. Preheat the oven to 350F or 180C.
2. Prepare 2 baking trays, grease or cover with silicone paper.
3. In a deep dish, mix the 1/4 cup of sugar with the cinnamon and stir well. 4. In a medium bowl, sift the flour, baking powder and chocolate powder. Add the salt and mix well.
5. In another bowl with the blender, beat the butter with the cup and a half of sugar for 3-5 minutes, until it is very creamy and pale.
6. Add eggs 1 to 1, beating in between until fully incorporated. Add the vanilla, beat.
7. Add the mixture of flour and chocolate and beat at low speed, only until mixing, not overbatting. Finish mixing by hand.
8. With an ice cream portioner, form pellets of dough of about 2-3 cm. of diameter and roll in the mixture of cinnamon and sugar.
9. Place in the can of the oven separated by at least 7 cm. each other.
10. Bake for 15 minutes, the edges will be crispy and the center still soft.
11. Remove from the oven, let stand 5 minutes in the can and then move to a tray until they are completely cooled.
12. Store in airtight container.

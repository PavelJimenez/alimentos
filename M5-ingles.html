Ingredients

500 g of mango
250 g of sugar
1 lemon

Elaboration

1. First, peel and chop the mango. Then we weigh the pulp without skin or bones. My mango weighed 500 g so I will use 250 g of sugar, since you have to use half the amount of sugar as of fruit.
2. Then we wash and squeeze a lemon.
3. Put the chopped mango, 250 g of sugar and the juice of a lemon in a pot. We stir the mixture a bit and cook it over medium heat for about 20 minutes. You have to remove the jam from time to time to avoid sticking to the bottom of the pan. After 20 minutes, the fruit will have been quite tender, so we can put out the fire.
4. Once we have removed the jam from the fire, we must pass the blender to achieve a homogeneous texture.
5. Finally, you have to boil a couple of glass jars in a pot to sterilize them and pour the jam inside. We must be very careful not to burn ourselves. Then close the jars well and put them face down until they cool down.
6. Now you can store this delicious homemade mango jam in a cool, dry place away from sunlight. This will keep it in good condition for about six months. Remember that, once opened, you have to store the jam in the refrigerator or refrigerator to keep it properly.

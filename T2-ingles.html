Ingredients

For the cake

160 grams of Flour
100 grams of Butter without salt
50 grams of Sugar glass
1 egg yolk unit
5 milliliters of Vanilla Essence
30 grams of strawberry jam
300 grams of Strawberries

For the pastry cream

400 milliliters of whole milk
100 grams of Sugar glass
50 grams of Corn starch
1 unit of Egg
1 egg yolk unit
5 milliliters of Vanilla Essence

Elaboration

1. List all the ingredients for the strawberry cake.
2. In a bowl mix the flour and butter with the fingertips (cut into cubes and cold), until it forms fine crumbs.
3.Add the icing sugar to the mixture and continue stirring with your fingers.
4.Add the yolk and mix to form a soft dough, if necessary add a little water.
5.Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for 1 hour.
6. Stir the dough with a rolling pin, arrange in the mold, prick the base with a fork, cover with wax paper, add counterweight (such as legumes) and bring to the oven at 180 Â° C for 20 minutes.
7. Remove from the oven, remove the counterweight, leftover the dough from the edges and bake 5 minutes more.
8. After that time, dissolve the strawberry jam in 1 tablespoon of water and spread the base of the cake.
9.For the pastry cream, in a bowl mix the egg yolk, the egg, the powdered sugar and the cornstarch. Boil the milk with the vanilla essence, remove from the heat when it begins to bubble and add the warm milk to the egg mixture, beat all the time. Return the cream to the pot over medium heat, stirring constantly, when the mixture is thick reduce the heat and cook 3 minutes more. Add the cream to a bowl, cover with plastic wrap to avoid a coating.
10.Pour the pastry cream in the mold where we have the broken dough smeared with jam, spread well. Arrange the cut strawberries in slices on the cream.
11.Pince the strawberries with the jam, loosen the strawberry cake when it is well curdled and consume the same day, although it can be kept refrigerated overnight.

Ingredients

Base: 1 pack of cookies mary
1 package of cinnamon-covered cookies (another package of cookies can be used, but this gives it a better taste
) 30g of sugar
50g of butter
1 egg

Filling: 250g of cajeta
380g of cream cheese (2 bars)
3 eggs
500ml evaporated milk
500ml whole milk
100g of sugar
50g of butter

Elaboration: 1-First, place the package of cookies Mary and cinnamon cookies in a tight plastic bag and close it.
2-Then, with the help of a rolling pin, break the cookies until they are powdered. The same can go through a food processor as another way to break them, the point is that they are ground to make a dough with them. 3-Then, in a bowl, place the cookies, 1 egg, 30g of sugar and 50g of butter and mix everything until you get a firm dough.
4-After, in a container for pie, place the dough in a 1cm thick layer, taking care that the layer is of uniform height.
5-Now, in the blender place 250g of cajeta with 380g of cream cheese, 500ml of evaporated milk and 500ml of whole milk, 3 eggs, 100g of sugar and 50g of melted butter and liquefy all to form a homogeneous mixture. > 6-After, pour the mixture on the cookie crust in the standing bowl.
7-Now, bake for 30 minutes at 180ºC.
8- Finally, let cool and enjoy!

Ingredients 2 cups flour 2 tablespoons of baking powder ½ teaspoon of salt ½ cup of vegetable oil ¾ cups of milk 8 ounces pineapple in drained syrup 3 eggs 1 teaspoon vanilla extract 1 ½ cups of sugar
preparation 1. Preheat the oven to 200ºC 2. Combine all dry ingredients, except sugar; reservation. 3. Apart, beat the oil, eggs, and vanilla, add the sugar and mix. Add the pineapple and mix again. 4. Add the mixture of dry ingredients and beat until incorporated. 5. Fill 3/4 of 8 cupcake cups with the mixture and bake for 18-20 minutes or until golden brown.

Ingredients

3 cups chopped pineapple in cubes
1/4 cup water
2-4 tablespoons granulated sugar

Elaboration:

Place the pieces of pineapple in the blender together with the water and blend with the "pulse" function about 3-4 times at intervals of 3 seconds each time. Empty the pineapple in a small casserole, add the sugar and heat over medium-high heat until it begins to "boil". Once it boils, continue cooking at low heat until it has a thick consistency. Make sure you constantly move with a spatula or spoon to avoid burning or caramelizing. When it reaches the desired consistency, remove from heat and empty into a jar. Let cool and enjoy smeared on a slice of bread.

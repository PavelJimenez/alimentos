Ingredients

1 cup cream cheese with pineapple two pine

FOR THE CREPAS:

¾ cup of milk
2 eggs
1 ½ tbsp. of sugar
½ cdita of salt
2 ½ tbsp. of melted butter Two Pines
¼ cup of rum
½ cdita Vanilla
½ cup flour

TO DECORATE:

½ cup of chocolate syrup

Elaboration:

1. In a blender add milk and eggs, blend for 10 seconds. Add sugar, salt, butter, rum and vanilla, blend for 10 more seconds. Finally add the flour mix with a spoon. Blend to a smooth paste.
2. Heat oil in a nonstick skillet. Pour a little of the mixture and move the bread so that the mixture covers the entire bottom.
3. Cook until you see small bubbles in the crepe.
4. When browned underneath turn the pancake over and cook for another minute.
5. Continue the procedure until the mixture of the crepes is finished.
6. To serve: fill with a portion of the cream cheese with pineapple, roll and decorate.

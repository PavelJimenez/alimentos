Ingredients:

500 g of ripe peaches
200 g of granulated sugar
1 Pinch of salt
Bottles for packaging

Elaboration

Wash the jars with boiling water or go through the dishwasher.
Wash and dry the peaches, traditionally peel, but I do it with skin. Chop the peaches into small pieces, discard the stones.
Weigh, add half the weight of the peaches in granulated sugar, stir and let stand refrigerated overnight, stir occasionally. The next day cook it or give it a point as it was said before. In a medium saucepan place the peaches with the sugar and the pinch of salt, cook over high heat until it begins to bubble, lower the heat so that it only boils gently. Remove the foam that forms and throw away. From the moment it boils, cook for 15-20 minutes until it has the desired texture.
To try and take a teaspoon to a plate and refrigerate it for a few minutes, try cold.
Pack hot carefully, cover, let cool and refrigerate or freeze.

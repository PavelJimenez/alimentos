Ingredients

2 1/4 cups of creams
3 cups of flour
1/2 cup of sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons of baking powder
1 pinch of salt
2 unsalted butter bars cut into pieces
2 beaten eggs
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
5 cups strawberry slices
3 tablespoons of sugar glass
vanilla bitumen

Elaboration

1.Preheat the oven. In a bowl, mix the flour, three tablespoons of sugar, the baking powder and the pinch of salt. Add the butter into small pieces.
2. Mix with a fork in a bowl 3/4 cup of cream, eggs, vanilla and flour mixture. Only until the ingredients are moist, not over robes.
3. Grab the dough and form a circle, put it on a baking sheet. Add 2 tablespoons of cream and sprinkle a spoonful of sugar.
4. Bake until it looks golden brown about 25 minutes. Let it cool completely.
5. Mix the strawberries with the fourth cup of sugar left for an hour.
6.With an electric mixer beat the cream with the remaining sugar to the point of nougat until it forms peaks. Once the cake is completely cold, cut in half to half add the cream and the sliced strawberries. Re-cover with the other layer of cake and on the top put the vanilla bitumen and the mixture of macerated strawberries.

Ingredients

200 grams of flour
½ teaspoon baking powder
150 grams of butter, softened
1 whole egg
125 grams of sugar
1 orange, its zest and half of its juice
1 egg yolk, lightly beaten

Elaboration

1. Preheat the oven to medium heat, 175 ° C, and grease and flour a baking sheet.
2. Sift the flour together with the baking powder into a large bowl, form a well in the center and add the butter, egg, sugar, grated zest and orange juice. Knead with your hands until you have a homogeneous and manageable paste.
3. Spread the dough with the rolling pin on a flat and floured surface until it reaches a thickness of just under ½ centimeter. Cut the cookies with a cutter, place them on the greased trays and varnish them with the egg yolk. Bake until golden brown, 10 to 12 minutes.

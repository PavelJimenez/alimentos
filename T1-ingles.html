Ingredients

Soletilla biscuits (about 12, those that fit in the mold)
2 pineapple cans in their juice (About 420 gr for a cake)
1 liter of liquid cream for riding
2 envelopes of pineapple jelly (Each sachet contains 85 grams)

Elaboration

1. Drain the juice from one of the pineapple tins and wet the cakes in the juice, put them in the base of a removable mold and reserve in the refrigerator.
2. We reserve a few slices of pineapple and the rest we crush well with the blender and semimontamos the cream.
3.The rest of the juice is put in a saucepan to boil, when it starts to boil add the gelatin, mix well and separate from the heat, let it temper a little, about five minutes or so.
4. We only have to mix the three preparations that we had reserved, the crushed pineapple, the juice with the gelatin and the cream.
5. We pour the mixture on the base of sponge cakes and keep in the refrigerator, it is best to prepare this cake the day before. Before serving unmold and garnish with the pineapple slices we had reserved.

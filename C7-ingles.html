Ingredients note: Ingredients for approximately 15 crepes:
4 eggs M.
175 g of wheat flour
65 g of chocolate powder
600 ml of whole milk
150 g of sugar
25 g of butter to melt
A pinch of salt
Unto or failing butter from Central Lechera Asturiana

Elaboration: 1. Beat the eggs with the sugar.
2. Add the milk, which should be at room temperature.
3. Add the flour, the pinch of salt, the chocolate and the melted butter. Help yourself with the blender to integrate all the ingredients well.
4. Heat a frying pan, top it with grease and if you do not have it, an excellent substitute is the Central Lechera Asturiana butter, it has an intense flavor that enriches the final result. Pour the dough little by little with a saucepan making sure that it is well spread all over the surface. The crepe has to be done on both sides, so to know if it is ready, lift the dough with a fork and see that it has a golden color. It is time to turn it around and do it on the opposite side. This step is usually done with your fingers, carefully grasping the ends of the dough, so be careful not to burn yourself!

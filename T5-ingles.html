Ingredients

1 Masa Brisa BUITONI
250 g (1 tablet) of Black Chocolate NESTLÉ Desserts
300 ml of whipping cream
125 g of butter

preparation

1. Preheat the oven to 200º.
2. Extend the mass of the breeze in a mold that can be removed. Cut out the leftover dough.
3. Puncture the bottom several times with a fork and cover it with wrapping paper, pressing the walls well. Fill the cake with dried chickpeas.
4. Bake 15 min, remove the chickpeas and paper and bake 5 min more, you have cooked.
5. While it cools, prepare the chocolate cream:
6. Heat the cream and when it starts to boil, remove the saucepan from the heat and add the butter and chopped chocolate.
7. Stir until you leave a thin cream.
8. Fill the cake.
9. Refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving. Decorate to taste.

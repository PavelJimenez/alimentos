Ingredients

1 package of cream cheese (190 gr) ½ cup of sugar 3 eggs 1 bar of melted butter (90 gr) 1 Teaspoon vanilla extract 1 cup of wheat flour ½ teaspoon baking powder ½ cup of blackberry jam
For the bitumen: 1 butter bar (90 gr) 2 packages of cream cheese (190 gr each) 8 tablespoons of sugar glass Chocolate granillo to decorate

preparation:

PRE-HEATED OVEN AT 180ºC 1. Acrema in cheese until smooth. Add the sugar and integrate. Add the eggs one by one and then the melted butter. Beat until you have a uniform mixture. 2. Add the vanilla with flour and baking powder. Beat for 3 more minutes. 3. Pour the mixture in molds for shortcakes with saucers. Bake for 30 minutes. Cool and reserve. 4. With the help of a disheartener, remove the center of each pancake and reserve only the tapas. 5. Place the jam in a pastry bag and fill the pancakes almost to the top. Cover with the covers. 6. For the bitumen, add the butter with the cheese and the sugar until it is a smooth mixture. Place the bitumen in a pastry bag with curly duya and cover the cup cakes. Decorate with a bit of granillo. 7. Serve and offer.

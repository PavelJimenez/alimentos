Ingredients

1¾ cups of burned cajeta or vanilla cajeta
(525 g) 1½ cups of milk
(360 ml) ¼ cup aged rum
(60 ml) 4 tablespoons of butter
(60 g) 24 crepes ½ cup walnuts (60 g) chopped

Elaboration:

1. Boil the cajeta with the milk and remove it from the fire.
2.Add the aged rum and rest.
3. Melt 1 teaspoon of butter in a pan and heat 2 crepes on both sides.
4. Reserve the hot crepes and repeat the operation with the rest of the crepes and butter.
5. Fold the crepes in four, place them on individual plates and bathe them with the cajeta sauce; decorate with chopped nuts.

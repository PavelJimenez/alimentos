Ingredients

250gr of butter
300gr of sugar
3 eggs
350gr flour for biscuits
100gr of natural cut strawberry
4 tablespoons of milk
2 teaspoon of baking powder
Half a teaspoon of vanilla essence

Ingredients for frosting

300gr of butter
300gr of icing sugar
Half a teaspoon of vanilla essence
Natural strawberry

preparation

1.Preheat the oven to 180 Â° C.
2.In a bowl beat for two minutes and with the help of some electric rods the 250gr of butter along with the 300gr of sugar to obtain a light and frothy texture. While stirring, add the eggs little by little and one at a time, and then the 4 tablespoons of milk.
3.In another bowl, sift the 350gr flour, the half teaspoon of vanilla essence, the 2 teaspoons of baking powder and the 100g of chopped natural strawberry and incorporate it slowly into the mixture. Stir until you get a homogeneous mass.
4.Divide the dough in the molds and bake for 15 or 20 minutes, or until the dough rises and remains consistent.
Let the cupcakes cool.

How to prepare strawberry frosting

1.In a saucepan over low heat add the essence of lemon and the 300gr of butter. Once the mixture is melted add the 300gr of icing sugar. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir the mixture well. Let it cool until it has enough consistency to be able to smear it. Decorate the cupcakes with whole or sliced strawberries.
